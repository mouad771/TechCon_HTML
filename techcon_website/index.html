TechCon 2024 was a dynamic gathering of innovators, researchers, and industry leaders, held from September 8 to 10 at the Renaissance Austin Hotel in Texas. Over the course of the conference, attendees engaged in over 200 poster sessions and technical presentations, exploring cutting-edge topics in semiconductors, hardware, and emerging technologies. One of the highlights was the keynote by Deirdre Hanford, CEO of Natcast, who spoke about the evolving role of R&D and sustainability in the semiconductor industry. The event also featured CareerConnections sessions, enabling networking between students and leading tech companies.
